Shipping Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

At Black Haven, we are committed to delivering your orders safely and efficiently. This Shipping Policy outlines our shipping process, delivery timelines, and related information.

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 3-5 business days after order confirmation and payment verification. Processing time does not include shipping time. You will receive a confirmation message via WhatsApp once your order has been shipped.

Shipping Methods & Delivery Time

We partner with reliable courier services to ensure timely delivery. Shipping times may vary based on your location:

Location Estimated Delivery Time
Delhi NCR 2-4 business days
Major Metro Cities 4-6 business days
Other Cities & Towns 6-10 business days
Remote Areas 10-15 business days

Note: Delivery times are estimates and may be affected by factors beyond our control, such as weather conditions, public holidays, or courier delays.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated based on:

  • Order value
  • Delivery location
  • Product weight and dimensions

Free Shipping is available on orders above ₹2,500 within India.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via WhatsApp. You can use this tracking number to monitor your shipment's progress on the courier's website.

Shipping Restrictions

Currently, we only ship within India. International shipping is not available at this time.

We do not ship to P.O. Box addresses. Please provide a complete street address with a landmark for smooth delivery.

Incorrect Address

Please ensure your shipping address is accurate and complete before placing an order. We are not responsible for orders delivered to incorrect addresses provided by the customer. If you need to update your address after placing an order, contact us immediately via WhatsApp at +91 70114 03207.

Failed Delivery Attempts

If the courier is unable to deliver your order due to:

  • Incorrect or incomplete address
  • Recipient unavailability
  • Refusal to accept delivery

The package will be returned to us. Reshipment will incur additional shipping charges, which will be borne by the customer.

Damaged or Lost Packages

In the rare event that your order arrives damaged or is lost during transit, please contact us immediately at info@blackhaven.co.in or via WhatsApp. We will work with the courier service to resolve the issue and arrange for a replacement or refund.

Order Cancellation

You may cancel your order before it is shipped. Once the order has been dispatched, cancellation is not possible. In such cases, you may return the item as per our Returns & Exchange Policy.

Have Questions About Shipping?

If you have any questions or concerns about your shipment, feel free to reach out to us:

Email: info@blackhaven.co.in

WhatsApp: +91 70114 03207
